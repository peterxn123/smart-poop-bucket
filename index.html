Calibration Instructions

  1. Remove all objects from the scale and press Tare.
  2. Place a known weight (e.g. 500g or 1000g) on the scale.
  3. Enter the weight below and press Calibrate.
  4. Wait for confirmation. Your scale is now calibrated!
